What is Apollo?

Apollo is a work in progress, and is designed to simplify the connection of any backend data source to any client. Here's what it will do for you:




Give your database a break

Apollo will improve your app performance by enabling GraphQL functionality on your existing data architecture. Transfer only the data you need!

Speed up development

Simplify complicated backend data systems by decoupling it from your front-end code, allowing developers to build apps faster.

Boost performance

Apollo will unlock the ability to analyze and improve performance in the data-layer.
